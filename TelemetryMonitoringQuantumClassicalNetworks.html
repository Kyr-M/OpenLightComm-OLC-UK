Quantum Networks

OLC has developed a telemetry and monitoring suite for monitoring, analysis, and decision-making on both quantum and optical networks. The suite allows to: a) collect data using telemetry from a set of SDN Agents located on both quantum and optical devices; b) render these data available via a suitable data broker, e.g. Apache Kafka; c) analyze the data in a central entity acting as the SDN controller; d) provide insight and generate alarms when needed; e) present raw data, post-processing and post-analysis data in a dashboard; f) provide recommendations when this is possible to support the decision making process to the involved stakeholders (network operation, quantum operators, service operators).

The suite is easily configurable and customizable to meet the requirements stakeholders may have (e.g. granularity of collection, analysis, decision making, sensitivity of generated alarms).
