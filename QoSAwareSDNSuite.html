A QoS-aware SDN Suite for End-to-End Slices

We have developed a QoS-aware, SDN control-plane platform based on our unique, integrated, Metro-Access fixed-line architecture that exploits coordinated TDM-PON technologies in the two segments to avoid the costly alternative of over-provisioning of connections which are under-utilized.

The End-to-End architecture employes either integrated or disaggregated TDM-PON technologies which are suitably deployed in Metro and Access segments. The arrangement is orchestrated by our EU awarded SDN control-plane technology.LINK

This unique solution offers QoS-aware end-to-end slices featuring a dynamic bandwidth allocation with a service set-up/restoration time of < 8 seconds which manifests a two orders of magnitude improvement compared to what traditional NMS systems are allowing.
