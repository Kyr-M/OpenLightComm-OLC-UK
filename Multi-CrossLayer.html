Multi-layer and Cross-layer Networking for 6G

The selection of the appropriate technology, the automation of system’s operations as well as the corresponding performance optimization require the synergy and integration between several conceptual and knowledge building blocks. Essentially this is a multi-layer operation so the integration of different scope algorithms is necessary.

We capitalize on the extensive know-how being accumulated for over two decades and we apply the expertise gained to address these multi-facet challenges to provide novel and innovative softwarization solutions. Using the appropriate abstraction models that balance efficiency, accuracy and computational speed and we have been able to provide state-of-the-art software network management modules as in here
