Quantum Key Control and Management Layer Suite

The Quantum Key Control and Management Layer Suite is fully based on ITU-T recommendations and ETSI standardization guidelines and it consists of a Key Management Module (KMM), which is developed as a RESTful Web Service, and a QKD controller that allows to scale the geographic extent of QKD transportation via key-relay. OLC’s QKCM suite supports the following functions:

OLC has distilled and expanded the experience gained from the participation in world-class research projects on Softwarized and is offering SDN-enabled suites based on open-software

  • Key authentication, storage, protection, identification

  • Key provision to applications on request

  • Key replacement on request

  • Key destruction (based on decided key lifetime)

  • Management of the key pool

  • Allocation of keys to applications based on the agreed QoS performance

  • Synchronisation between KMM entities for key exchange

  • Key relay

The robustness and the scalability of the QKCM suite is verified via demos at BT’s premises in Adastral Park.

The ability of the KMM to pass on features of QKD security to suitable PUF terminals has also been demonstrated via a field experiment in conjunction with ANGOKA’s NZA PUF technology
