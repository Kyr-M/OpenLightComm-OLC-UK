Quantum Networks

The second quantum technology revolution is underway bringing fundamental changes in the way we perceive and implement security, telemetry and monitoring in Telecommunications networks. Moreover, quantum technology is opening up new pathways in sensing, timing, and measurement accuracy in the context of beyond-5G applications. To materialize this, intense research efforts are made worldwide to implement Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and Quantum Computing efficiently and cost-effectively.

OLC has distilled and expanded the experience gained from the participation in world-class research projects on Softwarized and is offering SDN-enabled suites based on open-software

  • Quantum Key Control and Management Layer Suite
  • Telemetry and Monitoring Suite for Quantum and Classical Networks
