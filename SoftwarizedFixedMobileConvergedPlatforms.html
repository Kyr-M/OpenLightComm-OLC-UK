Softwarized Fixed-Mobile Converged Platforms

SDN allows to integrate and operate heterogeneous technologies with no vendor-locked s/w and to accelerate innovation and service creation. This applies to both brown-field and green-field deployments extending the applicability of these technologies to new and/or unforeseen use-cases.
